Rohit Sharma, born on April 30, 1987, in Bansod, Nagpur, India, is a renowned cricketer and the vice-captain of the Indian national cricket team in limited-overs formats. Known for his elegant and aggressive batting style, Sharma has established himself as one of the finest opening batsmen in international cricket. He made his debut for the Indian team in 2007 and quickly gained recognition for his talent. Rohit Sharma holds the unique distinction of being the only player in the world to have scored three double centuries in One Day Internationals (ODIs). His exceptional leadership skills were acknowledged when he was appointed as the captain of the Indian cricket team in the absence of Virat Kohli on several occasions. Beyond his cricketing prowess, Sharma is celebrated for his calm demeanor and strategic approach on the field, making him a key figure in Indian cricket.
